About

AI Should Work For You

Not the other way around. We're building the future of personal AI — open, private, and endlessly extensible.

Our Vision

Most AI coding tools today are cloud-dependent, vendor-locked, and opaque. They send your code to external servers, charge monthly fees for basic functionality, and give you zero control over the model or behavior.

We believe AI assistants should be personal. They should run locally when possible, remember your preferences, learn from your coding style, and never compromise your privacy. That's why we built PersonalJARVIS.

JARVIS isn't just an autocomplete tool — it's an AI operating system that reads files, writes code, runs commands, manages projects, and adapts to how you work. And it's 100% open source.

Principles

What We Believe

🔓

Open Over Closed

Every line of code is MIT-licensed. Fork it, modify it, sell it. We believe the best developer tools are community-driven and transparent.

🏠

Local Over Cloud

Your data stays on your machine. We prioritize local execution with Ollama and only use cloud AI when you explicitly choose to — and when the task demands it.

🧩

Extensible Over Monolithic

JARVIS is a platform, not a product. Skills, plugins, MCP connectors — extend it to do anything. If we haven't built it, you (or the community) can.

🧠

Smart Over Expensive

Tiered inference routes simple tasks to free local models. You only pay for cloud AI when it genuinely matters. Most workflows cost pennies, not dollars.

🛡️

Safe Over Fast

Security isn't optional. Capability-based permissions, blocked destructive commands, and full audit logging come built-in — not as an afterthought.

🤝

Honest Over Hype

We don't use dark patterns, hidden fees, or misleading marketing. If something doesn't work yet, we say so. If it's experimental, we label it.
